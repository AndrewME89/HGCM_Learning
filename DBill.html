Direct Bills

Direct Billing is an arrangement between the hotel and company where it can be invoiced at the end of every month. In HMS, we can directly send the invoice to be sent to the guest company.

On HMS, check in instructions should state DBA / DBC. This stands for Direct Booking Agent or Direct Booking Company.

Please follow instructions very carefully as some incidentals the company will be covering for (eg. breakfast, parking)

Anything that is not covered by company, the guest must pay directly to the hotel.

To charge a Direct Bill on HMS:
  1. Double click on the guest profile and on the top right hand corner there should be a Chk Out Master button, please click and a pop up window will come up, click OK

  2. The check out folio should automatically appear, right click on Ledger #1 and choose payment

  3. Choose payment type as Direct Bill and automatically the voucher number should appear, DO NOT REMOVE THIS.

  4. Click NEXT and this should automatically charge the company and you can then check it out of the system

  5. If there is no voucher number provided, you can just put “.” & check it out of HMS

  6. There should be a chargeback attached to the guest registration card. Please write the room number on the top of paper and put it away along with all the other invoices in the Direct Bill pigeon hole

Troubleshooting Direct Bills

If the voucher number is incorrect or possible incorrect company profile had been loaded to HMS, you will come across CREDIT STOP OR CASH ONLY OR EXCEED LIMIT OVERRIDING

Credit stop or Cash Only stands for if the incorrect company was loaded to the guest profile or perhaps the company’s credit limit has gone over the amount.

To troubleshoot CREDIT STOP OR CASH ONLY, first look at the guest profile on HMS:

Does the company name match Booking Agent and Company tab?

Booking Agent can be found on the bottom right corner of HMS

Company tab can be found at the top left corner of HMS

To troubleshoot EXCEED LIMIT OVERRIDING, you can override this by just putting “.” in the voucher number & check it out of HMS

If for any reason, you are unable to charge and check out the guest on HMS. You will need to inform your Duty Manager or Reservations Team (Mon – Fri 9AM – 5PM)& make a virtual account of the reservation and transfer all the charges over to the new virtual folio and check out the original reservation once all ledgers are showing as $0.00.
