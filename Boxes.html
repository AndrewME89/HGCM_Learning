Safety Deposit Box Procedure

Safety deposit boxes are available free of charge at Reception for the safekeeping of guests’ personal belongings.

Procedure

When a guest requests the use of a safety deposit box, they are required to fill out the safety deposit box form. (The form is located in the pigeon hole on the left most side of the reception desk)

Requirements to be completed for Safety Deposit Box:

It is the responsibility of the staff member to ensure that the guest fills the following information on the Safety Deposit Box form:

  • Their name

  • Room number

  • Residential Address

  • Signatures of each person authorised to access the box.

  • File form with the guests Registration Card in the file box.

Master Key to Safety Deposit Box:

The master key for safety deposit box is kept out the front on the safety deposit boxes

Distribution of a Safety Deposit Box:
  1. Select a box that has a key in the lock.

  2. Remove the box from the slot and hand the grey box to the guest to insert their items

  3. It is preferred that you do not handle the goods if possible.

  4. When the guest is completed with insertion of goods, place the box back in the safety deposit vault.

  5. Issue the guest with the Safety Deposit Box key, ensure you explain to the guest that they are now reasonable for that key and as it’s a safety deposit box it is the only one we have.

    6. Note:

    If the key is lost the guest will be charged any and all locksmithing fees.

Alternative Storage of Goods for Safekeeping:

As an alternative, the hotel will also provide a space in our Front Office safe. If a guest chooses this option the Duty Manager must record this in the Duty log and put notes referring to this in the booking

Accessing the Safety Deposit Box:
  1. Request the guest to confirm the name and room.

  2. Retrieve the registration card and cross check the name and room number are correct

  3. The guest is to present their key to access the vault.

  4. The Safety Deposit Box form must be signed each time the guest accesses the safety deposit box.

  5. Ensure Signatures Match
