Lost Property Procedure

Occasionally, Guests may forget items which will need to be collected or sent back to them.

The following Procedure is to be followed when addressing Lost Property.

If the item was found inside the room:

  1. Any items found in departing rooms that are NOT in the rubbish bins OR are not hotel property must be brought down to housekeeping office and logged as Lost Property.

  2. If the item is a valuable item (i.e: money, gadgets, wallet, passport/ID, jewellery, watches), room attendant must call the housekeeping supervisor immediately.

  3. The Housekeeping supervisor is to collect the item from the room, log it in the Lost Property Log, and hand it to DM.

  4. All items need to be placed in a plastic bag.

  5. A Lost Property slip should be filled in and attached to the bag.

  6. Place the bag in the lost property locker in the luggage storage area.

The Lost Property Log can be found in M:\Operations\Front Office\Duty Managers\Lost Property

All items must be logged in the Lost Property spreadsheet with the correct information by the end of the day.

Where valuable items are involved, the Duty Manager will attempt to contact the guest and arrange collection or postage.

Items are stored in one of three places:

  • Housekeeping storage – All items. (Alcohol to be stored in a locked cupboard).

  • Back office safe – Valuable items.

  • Arrivals Room – Items to be collected or posted.

No items should be left behind in the back office.

All items (except perishable/valuable) will be stored for 3 months, including the current month. After 3 months, the items will be disposed of at the discretion of the hotel.

We do NOT store any opened food or drinks.

Any unopened drinks will be stored in housekeeping office for 30 days and will be disposed at the discretion of the hotel.

Guests enquiring about missing items are required to provide the following:

  • Reservation name

  • Departure date

  • Room number

  • Description of the item

Items can be either collected by guest at the hotel or posted at a cost

  • Advise the postage fee

  • Confirm the postal address

  • Confirm the credit card on file or send credit card authorisation form or CC bank details

  • Confirm the email address and contact number

All domestic postage by Australia Post standard parcel post (3+ business day). Fees are size dependent.

  • Small parcel bag – $12

  • Medium parcel bag – $15

  • Large parcel bag – $20

International postage is dependent on size, location and method of postings.

Fragile items must be wrapped with bubble wrap or brown paper to avoid damage.

Use the correct prepaid postage bag and fill in all the required information.

Send acknowledgement email to the guest after posting – including the tracking number and postal address. Please CC DM.

Post the payment in HMS under POSTAGE code and ‘Lost Property Postage Fee’ as remark.

Update the lost property log - Write down the tracking number in the remark section.

All items (except valuables) will be disposed of after 3 months. Please wear gloves when handling lost property.

Valuable items will be handed to nearest police station with the following info:

  • Reservation name

  • Departure date

  • Room number

If guests request for item to be disposed, please update the log and put a remark signifying so.

It is the responsibility of the Executive Housekeeper to dispose and maintain lost property items.

If a guest calls and we have not found the item me must record it in the spreadsheet labelled ‘enquiry tab’ to say what they have left in the room and a contact number in case we do find the item.
