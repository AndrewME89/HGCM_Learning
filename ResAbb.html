Reservation Abbreviations

The Special Instructions tab in a guest folio is where you will find all the information you may need about a booking.

Inclusions, charges to be transferred and type of payment are all shown in the Special Instructions using abbreviations.

Examples:

POA 170 RO: Guest will Pay Own Account (POA) at $170 p/night. This covers only the cost of the room. A Bond is required for this reservation.

POA 170 B1: Guest will Pay Own Account (POA) at $170 p/night with Breakfast included for 1 guest. A Bond is required for this reservation.

CC 475/544 RO POE: Accommodation charges have been paid by Credit Card, either through a third-party website, or by CC Authorisation. The Guest is required to Pay Own Extras (POE). A Bond is required for this reservation.

CC 170 B1 M P POE: Accommodation charges, Breakfast for 1 guest, Meals & Parking have been paid by Credit Card, either through a third-party website, or by CC Authorisation. Alcohol is not covered. The Guest is required to Pay Own Extras (POE). A Bond is required for this reservation.

DB 170 B1 M P POE: Accommodation charges, Breakfast for 1 guest, Meals & Parking have been paid by Direct Bill. Alcohol is not covered. The Guest is required to Pay Own Extras (POE). A Bond is required for this reservation.

DB 170/180 RO POE: Accommodation charges have been paid by Direct Bill. The Guest is required to Pay Own Extras (POE). A Bond is required for this reservation.

DB 170 B1 AA: Accommodation charges and Breakfast for 1 guest have been paid by Direct Bill, though we are Awaiting Authorisation (AA). The Guest is required to Pay Own Extras (POE). A Bond is required for this reservation.

DB 170 B1 POE: DB 170/180 RO POE: Accommodation charges and Breakfast for 1 guest have been paid by Direct Bill. The Guest is required to Pay Own Extras (POE). A Bond is required for this reservation.

ACB 170/188: All charges are being taken care of by the company. A Bond is not required for this reservation.

FOC: Accommodation is Free Of Charge (FOC). The Guest is required to Pay Own Extras (POE).

FOC AC: No charges are to be made to this reservation.

The Room Type is to be entered under the Payment Instructions (CLASQ / CLASK / SUPQ / SUPK / PREM).

Occasionally, there will be extra notes associated with some bookings. For example, if Breakfast for one guest is included in the booking, but required for a second guest, Reservations will add a note in the payment instructions.

For example:

POA 176.22*2 B1
CLASQ - Additional breakfast to be charged at $22

In the above, one breakfast is covered as part of the booking. If another is required, it is to be charged at $22.00, instead of the standard rate.
