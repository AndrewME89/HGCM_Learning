Completing the Audit

The most important part of the Night Audit role is the Audit itself. It is crucial that everything in the venue is balanced; otherwise, the system cannot close the business day.

It is essential that you complete the Organizing the Black Folder step before continuing with the Audit.

In iHMS, select: Reports → Before Night Audit Reports → Transaction For Business Day

  1. Set Charge Date "Start of Range" to Today.

  2. Set Charge Date "End of Range" to Today.

  3. Change Select Postcode Range "Start of Range" to Accommodation.

  4. Change Select Postcode Range "End of Range" to Visa/Mastercard.

  5. Click OK.

  6. Print the entirety of this report.

Arrange these reports as follows:

To keep: To the Duty Manager: To the Day Bag:
American Express ALL ENCORE pages Everything else
Eftpos ALL FOOTLIGHTS pages
Visa / MasterCard ALL ROOM SERVICE pages EXCEPT "Supper" & "Delivery" pages
Any card payments Movies

Once the extra reports have been placed in the Day Bag pile, you can move it to the Back Office, under the label “Day Bag”, which is between the two computers.

Unstaple any payment receipts from the previous shift's Transaction for Business Day Reports, and place them neatly back on the pile they came from. Discard any previous Reports to avoid confusion, as you have just printed out the updated one.

Organize the payment receipts by room number as stated on the Transaction for Business Day Report and highlight the amount & transaction number after confirming the details are correct.

Neatly staple the payment receipts to the corresponding Transaction for Business Day Report page (AMEX receipts to the American Express page, etc).

Once completed, create a new Day Bag pile with the transactions sorted alphabetically (AR Pending on top, Visa/Mastercard at the bottom).
