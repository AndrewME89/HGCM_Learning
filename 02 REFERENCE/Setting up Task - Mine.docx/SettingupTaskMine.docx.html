SETTING UP TASK IN HMS

STANDARD

Tasks can be set up for guests’ requests or job or undertaking to be actioned either by an individual or department.

Example of task:

  • Roll Res
  • Set up bottle of sparkling prior to arrival
  • Follow up missing chargeback
  • Please place a foam pillow and bathrobes on arrival
  • Please remove foam pillow after guest departure
  • Following up payment

PROCEDURE

Setting Up Task

  1. Right click on the folio that requires a task.
  2. Click ‘Add Message’

  1. Set the type from Message to Task (using the drop down menu) and ensure Status is “Open”.

  1. Down further is the Action box.
  1. Choose the relevant department or team member that the task needs to be assigned to.
  2. Choose the correct date the task needs to be actioned on
  3. In the text box at the bottom – Write down the task required.

  1. Click ‘Next’ button
  2. The set task will appear in tab (6) Communication in the message section of the window.

  1. You will see the reservations now has a small Light Globe Icon  next to the name.

*Task can also be set up from tab (6) Communication*

  1. Right click on the message section on tab (6) window.

  1. Follow the above steps to set up a task.

Viewing Task

  1. Right click on the folio that has Light Globe Icon  next to the name.
  2. Click ‘View Message’.

  1. The below window will pop-up
  2. You can check who and when is the task being created by clicking on History
  3. Browse through different task under the same folio by utilising the arrows
  4. Click ‘Cancel’ to close the window.

*Tasks can also be viewed in message box in tab (6) Communication*

Closing Task

Once the task is done/actioned, the person responsible for it most close the task.

  1. Right click on the folio that has Light Globe Icon  next to the name.
  2. Click ‘View Message’.
  3. The below window will pop-up

  1. Change the status from open to close

  1. Click ‘Next to confirm’.

Printing Task List

  1. To print the task list, click Reports – Reservations – Task List by Action Date. Select todays (required date) days in both fields, select Active Status as Open and click OK.

  1. This will generate the task list.
