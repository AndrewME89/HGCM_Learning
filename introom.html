Interconnecting Rooms

Although we do not actively advertise interconnecting rooms on our website or OTAs, we can accommodate such requests, especially for families.

Room Configurations

All interconnecting rooms, except for 1609/1610 and 1709/1710, feature an external door that can be closed, creating a private hallway between rooms 06 and 07. These rooms do not have an internal connecting door. We can unlock the doors for rooms 06 and 07, enabling guest access without key cards if they choose to use the external door as their primary entrance. However, for safety reasons, this is generally not recommended.

Rooms 1609/1610 and 1709/1710 have an internal door connecting them.

Key Information
  • RED: These rooms cannot interconnect due to lack of a door, malfunctioning keys, or other issues.

  • PURPLE: Only one key is available for these interconnecting doors. Exercise caution when allocating. Lost/damaged keys require a locksmith, and the guest will be charged.

  • GREEN: These doors are functional with multiple keys. Provide guests with only one interconnecting door key.

Access with GM Keys

Two GM keys can unlock most interconnecting doors (except on Level 6). The key with a yellow tag, labeled "Master Key for Walk Thru," can also be used as a backup. It's located in the back office key cabinet. Additionally, the Duty Manager's master key set includes a GM key with a green cover.

Maintenance

Regularly check the door locks. If any are rusty or not functioning correctly, inform Andrew in Maintenance.
