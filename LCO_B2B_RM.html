Familiarise Yourself With Late Check Outs, Back To Backs & Room Moves

Awareness of late checkouts, back-to-back bookings, and room moves allows staff to anticipate guest needs and ensure smooth transitions. This minimizes disruptions and enhances the guest experience.

Being informed helps staff plan room cleaning and preparation efficiently, preventing delays in room availability and maximizing occupancy.

Procedure
  1. Shift Handover

    • At the start of each shift, carefully review information from the previous shift regarding:

      • Late checkouts

      • Back to back reservations

      • Any scheduled room moves

  2. Check Guest Folios

    • Thoroughly review guest folios for remarks indicating late checkout requests.

    • If a late checkout is approved, note the new departure time and communicate it to housekeeping and relevant departments.

  3. Back to Back Reservations

    • Identify rooms with back to back bookings.

    • Ensure these rooms have been addressed correctly (charges transferred, etc).

  4. Room Moves

    • Review any planned room moves.

    • Communicate changes to housekeeping and the front desk to ensure guests are directed to the correct rooms.

    • Update iHMS to reflect the room changes.

  5. Communication

    • Maintain open communication with all relevant departments, including housekeeping, front desk, and management.

    • Inform guests of any changes to their reservations or room assignments in a timely and professional manner.
