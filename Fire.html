Fire Safety Procedures

In the case of a Fire or Evacuation we are required to follow certain protocols.

It is important that we follow these procedures to ensure not only the safety of our patrons, but our own as well.

In the case of emergecies such as these, it is important to remember the chain of command, as different personnael have different roles in these events.

Chief Fire Warden

Duty Manager

 

Deputy Warden

Guest Service Agent (Main)

Maintenance Manager

Food & Beverage Supervisor

Stand By: Chef, Executive Housekeeper

 

Wardens

Food & Beverage Attendant

Reservations Agent

Administration Staff

Maintenance Handyperson

Housekeeping Supervisor

 

Kitchen Team

Ensure Gas Main is switched off at Emergency Switch

 

Housekeeping Attendants / Public Areas

Ensure store rooms are secure and evacuate building to muster point using fire stairs.

Assist guests and provide directions where necessary.

Below is a more in-depth breakdown of each persons role in an emergency situation:

Duty Manager (Chief Warden)

Proceed to Fire Panel

Check LCD screen for location of alarm activation - Do NOTgo up to investigate

The LCD screen will display the location of the alarm

Identify whether it is hotel or apartment side.

Identify the type of alarm - Fire or Sprinkler


Contact building Manager (0428 240 937), Head of Departments, supervisors including Houseperson (pm shift) OR direct wardens to make calls

Await Fire Brigade arrival at Fire Panel

Check and communicate with GSA to ensure all reception tasks are complete

Evacuate once the alarm sounds 'Whoop' 'Whoop”

Direct site wardens to fire exit points or complete the roll call

Wardens can also be directed to Fire Exits door to assist guests:

* Fire Exit near reception * Fire Exit in Footlights
* Fire Exit at the basement (Lonsdale St side)
* Fire Exit at the basement (Apartment side)


Send all the remaining staff to evacuation point with one of the contingency reports used to check guests and staff off

Assist in reinhabitation process (service recovery)

Acknowledge the situation and apologise for the inconvenience

Note down the room number

Print out 'Emergency Service Recovery' letter and complete letter drop

Offer bottle water, towels, late check out, etc. if needed (communicate with the team)

Recommend guests to sit down in the lobby, Encore, Footlights while waiting for lift


Advise AGM, EAM, Maintenance Manager & FOM

Complete incident report within 24 hours

Send to AGM , EAM, FOM, Maintenance Manager & Housekeeping Manager

OR

Announce False Alarm - with Fire Brigade approval - Over PA

Advise AGM, EAM, Maintenance Manager & FOM

Deputy Warden (GSA)


Print Contingencies


HMS Reports → Batch → In House - Fire


Lock down lifts


Grab the T9 key from key box in back office (if key box is locked, grab T9 key from DM float)


Proceed to the lifts and place the key in the key slot marked 'Fire Service' (with the red ring)


Turn the key to the 'On' position


All lifts will be called to the Reception level and doors opened


Open the Car Park Garage Door


Flick the key beside the intercom at the RHS of reception desk and leave it at that position.

Set the entrance glass door to open mode


Flick the key beside the intercom at the LHS of reception desk to OPEN mode and leave it at that position.

Prop open the fire door on the ground floor

Assist guests as needed & answer phone calls

Direct Guests to evacuation point (129 Lonsdale Street)

Await further instructions from Fire Brigade / DM

Assist in reinhabitation process


Acknowledge the situation and apologise for the inconvenience


Note down the room number (Second Effort Program in place)


Print out 'Emergency Service Recovery' letter and complete letter drop


Check with Duty Manager for any service recovery offering

Wardens

Report to Duty Manager

Assist guests as needed

Direct Guests to evacuation point

Await further instructions from Fire Brigade / DM

Wardens can also be directed to Fire Exits door to assist guests:

* Fire Exit near reception

* Fire Exit in Footlights* Fire Exit at the basement (Lonsdale St side)* Fire Exit at the basement (Apartment side)

At the evacuation point use one of the contingency reports to check guests and staff off

Assist in reinhabitation process

Acknowledge the situation and apologise for the inconvenience
