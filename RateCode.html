Rate Codes

The applicable rate for each room is determined by the initial quote provided to the guest and must be accurately reflected in the reservation.

Rate Code Definitions:
  • GOVBB: Government rate inclusive of breakfast.

  • GOVRO: Government rate, room only.

  • INTBARBB: Internet booking at Best Available Rate (flexible cancellation up to 24 hours before arrival), inclusive of breakfast.

  • INTBARRO: Internet booking at Best Available Rate (flexible cancellation up to 24 hours before arrival), room only.

  • INTSAVERBB: Internet booking at Saver Rate (non-refundable), inclusive of breakfast.

  • INTSAVERRO: Internet booking at Saver Rate (non-refundable), room only.

  • LEIBARBB: Leisure booking at Best Available Rate (flexible cancellation up to 24 hours before arrival), inclusive of breakfast. Use this rate code for private bookings and direct reservations when breakfast is included.

  • LEIBARRO: Leisure booking at Best Available Rate (flexible cancellation up to 24 hours before arrival), room only. Use this rate code for private bookings and direct reservations when breakfast is not included.

  • ZZCOMP: Reserved for complimentary rooms or virtual folios.
