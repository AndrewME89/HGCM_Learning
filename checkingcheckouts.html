Ensure That All Guests Have Checked Out By 11:00 AM / 12:00 PM

At approximately 11:15 AM, generate the Expected Departure Summary Report (Reports → Departures → Expected Departure Summary) to identify rooms scheduled for departure today.

  • For guests with approved late checkouts, note the requested departure time next to their room number on the report.

Follow-up on Unchecked Rooms

For any rooms still marked as occupied past the standard checkout time:

  1. Verify Non-B2B Status:

    • In HMS, set the date range from today to tomorrow.

    • Clear the search box and execute the search.

    • Sort the results by guest name and manually review each entry.

    • Pay close attention to "sharing guest" names, as some guests may use these for secondary bookings.

  2. Confirm Absence of Late Checkout Requests:

    • Manually access the folio for each occupied room.

    • Review both the "Remarks" section and "Checkout Instructions" for any recorded late checkout requests.

  3. Contact Guests:

    • Call guests in rooms due to depart that have passed verification steps 1 and 2.

    • Inquire about their anticipated departure time, given that it is past the standard checkout time.

    • If a late checkout is requested, note this on your list and inform both the Duty Manager and Housekeeping.

Unreachable Guests
  • The Duty Manager will conduct a bag check for rooms where guests cannot be contacted.
