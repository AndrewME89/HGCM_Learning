Standard Programs Quoting Tips
Booking.com, Expedia & GC Rewards members receive an additional 10% off the public rates. Utilise the HGC Website when quoting the secret deals to guests. By clicking on the "Promotions" tab and then HGCM it will load all of our secret deals.
Booking.com Business Bookers receive an additional 5% off BAR, Saver & AP 7 Remember guests can be members of particular OTA sites. Being a member of these sites allows guests to get an extra discount which we can always match.
