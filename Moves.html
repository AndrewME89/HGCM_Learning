Room Moves

Occasionally, a guest may request - or even require - a Room Move.

This could be due to wanting a larger space, or because there is some kind of fault within the room.

Procedure
  1. When room moving, please advise the guest of the following:

    • If they are going out for the day of room move, they have to pack all their stuff and leave it inside their room as we will be the one to transfer all their bags to the new room.

    • Before they leave the hotel, drop by the reception area and hand in the old key.

    • Inform that their new keys will be ready with us at the reception when they come back

  2. If room move has already been scheduled, make sure to rush the new room to housekeeping and make it a priority as soon as possible.

    • To check if the room move has already been scheduled, in HMS select: Reports- Housekeeping- Live Scheduled Room Change

    • Once the new room is ready, request for the Houseperson to move the guest’s belongings from their old room to the new one.

    • Update the room change in HMS by searching the guest name/room number and right click and choose Confirm Change Room.

      • Make sure that the room that you are moving them into is the actual room where you moved all their belongings.

    • Cut new keys and leave arrival key box for collection

    • Update the guest’s registration card with new room number and file it in the new room in reg card bin

Scheduling a Room Move

For room moves happening later in the day, or another day altogether, do the following:

  1. Go to tab (3) Room in the guest’s folio that requires a room move.

  2. Choose the date you want the room move to take place and click the three dot icon. This will bring up the rooming list.

    3. (I.E: room move to be scheduled on 16/09/2022 from room 205 to 206)

  3. Choose the new room and click OK.

  4. Put a dot ‘.’ And click OK for the below pop-up window

  5. This guest will be scheduled to move to the new room on the specified date.

Same Day Room Move
  1. Go to tab (3) Room in the guest’s folio that requires a room move.

  2. Choose the current date and click the three dot icon. This will bring up the rooming list.

    3. (I.E: room move to be scheduled from room 209 to 210 on the 14/09/22)

  3. Choose the new room and click OK.

  4. Put a dot ‘.’ And click OK for the below pop-up window

  5. The below window will pop up

    • YES – If the room is ready

    • No – Room is NOT ready and we would like to schedule it

  6. Click ‘OK’ for the below pop-up window

  7. Follow the room move procedure for the room not ready.

  8. Once the room is ready, confirm the room change.

Live Scheduled Room Change Report

To check if there is any room move has already been scheduled, in HMS select:

Reports- Housekeeping- Live Scheduled Room Change
