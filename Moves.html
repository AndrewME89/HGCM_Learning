Room Moves

Occasionally, a guest may request - or even require - a Room Move. This could be due to wanting a larger space, or because there is some kind of fault within the room.

Procedure

When room moving, please advise the guest of the following:

  • If they are going out for the day of the room move, they need to pack all their belongings and leave them inside their room, as we will transfer all their bags to the new room.

  • Before they leave the hotel, they should drop by the reception area and hand in their old key.

  • Inform them that their new keys will be ready at the reception when they return.

If a room move has already been scheduled, prioritize preparing the new room for housekeeping as soon as possible.

  • To check if the room move is scheduled, in HMS select: Reports → Housekeeping → Live Scheduled Room Change

Once the new room is ready, request the Houseperson to move the guest’s belongings from their old room to the new one.

  • Update the room change in HMS by searching the guest name/room number, right-clicking, and choosing Confirm Change Room.

  • Double-check that the room you are moving them into is the actual room where you moved all their belongings.

  • Cut new keys and leave them in the arrival key box for collection.

  • Update the guest’s registration card with the new room number and file it in the new room's registration card bin.

Scheduling a Room Move
For room moves happening later in the day, or another day altogether, do the following:
  1. Go to tab (3) Room in the guest’s folio that requires a room move.

  2. Choose the date you want the room move to take place and click the three-dot icon. This will bring up the rooming list.

  3. Choose the new room and click OK.

  4. Put a dot ‘.’ and click OK for the below pop-up window

This guest will be scheduled to move to the new room on the specified date.

Same Day Room Move
  1. Go to tab (3) Room in the guest’s folio that requires a room move.

  2. Choose the current date and click the three-dot icon. This will bring up the rooming list.

  3. Choose the new room and click OK.

  4. Put a dot ‘.’ and click OK for the below pop-up window

The below window will pop up:

  • YES – If the room is ready

  • No – Room is NOT ready and we would like to schedule it

Click ‘OK’ for the below pop-up window

  • Follow the room move procedure for the room not ready.

  • Once the room is ready, confirm the room change.

Live Scheduled Room Change Report

To check if any room moves have already been scheduled, in HMS select:

  • Reports → Housekeeping → Live Scheduled Room Change
