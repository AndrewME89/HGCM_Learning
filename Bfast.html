Preorder Breakfast Procedure

As a guest promotion, we offer Preorder Breakfast at a cheaper rate, providing the Guest aranges it with Reception before 10:00pm.

It is important that this procedure is followed correctly, to ensure there are no mistakes resulting in Guests missing out.

  1. Confirm how many guests will require Breakfast

  2. In the Additional Items tab, choose the correct Breakfast code:

    1. For one breakfast select: BFAST SPECIAL - BREAKFAST FOR 1 PRE-ORDER.

    2. For breakfast by the amount of people in the room select: BFAST SPECIAL - BREAKFAST FOR 2 PRE-ORDER

    For example, if a room with 3 people requires only one breakfast, you would use the BFAST SPECIAL - BREAKFAST FOR 1 PRE-ORDER stock code.

    However, if they required breakfast for all 3 guests, you would use the BFAST SPECIAL - BREAKFAST FOR 2 PRE-ORDER code.

  3. Check that the correct dates are shown in the Start Date & End Date sections.

    4. Generally, a guest will only order breakfast for 1 morning, but if they are staying for several days, the Additional Item will automatically cover the entirety of their stay.

    You must ensure that the correct dates are entered into the system to avoid extra charges to the guest.

    If the guest is ordering breakfast for multiple days, please make a note in the Remarks section of the guest folio to ensure there is no confusion.

    Note:

    When entering the dates for the preorder, ensure you have selected the day before the breakfast is to be consumed, as the system assumes the charge is for the next day.

The guest may choose to pay for their breakfast immediately, or at the end of their stay.

If paying straight away, process payment as you usually would.

Otherwise, simply close the guest folio after confirming all details are correct.
