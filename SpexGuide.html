Communications Guide

The Communications for a reservation is your guide to payment, inclusions and additional items for the booking.

It is imperative that you read and understand the Communications for every folio you Check In or Out.

The layout of all instructions must be as below and all instructions must be in uppercase:

PAYMENT METHOD RATE (INCLUSIONS) + ADDITIONAL ITEMS POE - BTP **PERM AUTH**
ROOM TYPE

Example:

  • Abbreviations should be in alphabetical order (e.g. B1INW)

  • The rate doesn’t require a ‘$’ sign

  • For multiple nights:

    • with same rate:

    • with different rates:

  • Anything in brackets is included in the rate:

    • $186 rate includes room and breakfast for 2

  • Any additional charges are to be added after:

    • Guest to pay extra $40 rollaway bed fee (not included in $210.60 rate)

    • Multiple payment method Communication:

      • Must be separated by ‘//’

    • First 2 nights have been prepaid and the last 2 nights are to be settled by the Guest.

Chargeback

If a company sends a CC authorisation or chargeback, it is important to update the following boxes:

  • Check In: Communication + Room type

  • Check Out: Late Check Out Time, Chargeback Restrictions, Email address for invoice, Pre-authorisation/Bond Information

  • Posting: further information regarding posting, charges, payment & instructions

Remarks – Pop-Up Notes:

This will be the notes that will initial pop-up when folio is opened. Important information and notes are added here by both FO & Res.

  • Each note added on remarks should include staff initial and date when notes had been added.

  • Any corresponding update on initial note added should be added under the note.
