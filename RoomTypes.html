Room Types

Classic Queen Room (1 Queen Bed)

Featuring one queen bed, an ensuite bathroom, and a sitting area with two tub chairs and a coffee table, the Classic Queen Room also offers a private walk-out "Juliet" balcony and a dedicated workspace. It's the perfect choice for a comfortable short business trip or a relaxing city getaway.

Classic King/Twin Room (1 King Bed or 2 Single Beds)

This room offers the flexibility of either one king-size bed or two king single beds. It also includes an ensuite bathroom, a sitting area with two tub chairs and a coffee table, a private walk-out "Juliet" balcony, and a workspace. Whether for business or leisure, the Classic King/Twin room provides a comfortable stay in Melbourne.

Superior Queen Room (1 Queen Bed)

Recently refurbished, the Superior Queen Room combines smart design with modern amenities. It features one queen bed, a dedicated workspace, a sitting area with two armchairs and a coffee table, and a fully renovated bathroom with a luxurious rain shower. Enjoy the latest entertainment on the new LCD TV with limited Foxtel channels, and step out onto your private "Juliet" balcony for fresh air and city views.

Superior King/Twin Room (1 King Bed or 2 Single Beds)

The Superior King/Twin Room offers the choice of one king-size bed or two king single beds, along with a host of modern amenities. This recently refurbished room is ideal for business travelers, featuring a dedicated workspace, a sitting area with two armchairs and a coffee table, and a fully renovated bathroom with a deluxe rain shower. Relax with entertainment on the new LCD TV with limited Foxtel channels or step out onto your private "Juliet" balcony to take in the city.

Premier King (1 King Bed)

Spacious and stylish, the Premier King Room provides a luxurious retreat. Enjoy the comfort of a king-size bed, a modern work area, and a chaise lounge. The room also boasts a 40" LCD TV and a brand-new bathroom with a relaxing rain shower and contemporary fixtures. All Premier King Rooms are front-facing, offering captivating views over Lonsdale Street and the surrounding cityscape.
