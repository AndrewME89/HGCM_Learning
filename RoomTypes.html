Room Types

Classic Queen Room (1 Queen Bed)

One queen bed, ensuite bathroom, sitting area with two tub chairs and coffee table, private walk out “Juliet” balcony and work space. The Classic Queen room is a comfortable way to spend that short business trip or casual getaway in Melbourne.

Classic King/Twin Room (1 King Bed OR 2 Single Beds)

Includes either one king size bed or two king single beds, ensuite bathroom, sitting area with two tub chairs and coffee table, private walk out “Juliet” balcony and work space. The Classic King/Twin room is a comfortable way to spend that short business trip or casual getaway in Melbourne.

Superior Queen Room (1 Queen Bed)

Smart and well appointment the Superior Queen room has been recently refurbished and contains one queen bed. Ideal for the corporate traveller it offers a dedicated work space, sitting area with two arm chairs and coffee table, featuring newly renovated bathroom with luxurious rain shower, new LCD TVs with limited Foxtel channels & your own private walk out “Juliet” balcony.

Superior King/Twin Room (1 King Bed OR 2 Single Beds)

Smart and well appointment the Superior King/Twin room has been recently refurbished and contains either one king size bed or two king single beds. Ideal for the corporate traveller it offers a dedicated work space, sitting area with two arm chairs and coffee table, fully renovated bathroom featuring deluxe rain shower, new LCD TVs with limited Foxtel channels & your own private walk out “Juliet” balcony.

Premier King (1 King Bed)

Spacious and stylish, the room offers a king size bed with modern work area and chaise lounge, 40” LCD TV and a brand-new bathroom featuring a relaxing rain shower and modern fixtures. All Premier King Rooms are front facing offering views over Lonsdale Street and the surrounds. A perfect getaway for the more discerning traveller.
