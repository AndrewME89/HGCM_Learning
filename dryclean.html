Dry cleaning Procedure

We offer convenient dry cleaning services to our hotel guests through our partner, Masters Dry Cleaning.

Key Information
  • Dry cleaning bags and forms are typically available in the guest's wardrobe drawers. If not, you can find them in the middle drawer at Reception.

  • Masters Dry Cleaning collects items around 8-9 AM daily, with next-day delivery.

  • For same-day service (by the end of the day or the night before), guests must drop off their laundry before 8:30 AM.

  • The hotel is not responsible for any damage incurred during the dry cleaning process.

  • Masters Dry Cleaning's contact number is 03 9399 4700.

Dropping Off Guest Laundry
  1. Before accepting laundry, ensure the form is completed accurately with the guest's room number, name, and signature. Verify that the number of items listed matches the actual items.

  2. Place the completed form inside the laundry bag and tie it securely. Place the bag in the designated hamper within the Housekeeping cupboard.

  3. For same-day service, inform the Duty Manager so they can notify Masters Dry Cleaning.

Receiving and Delivering Cleaned Laundry
  1. Upon receiving cleaned laundry from Masters Dry Cleaning, verify the room number and guest name against the attached receipt and the information in HMS.

  2. Photocopy the receipt and attach it to the corresponding guest's registration card.

  3. Request a Houseperson to deliver the laundry to the guest's room.

Posting the Charge
  1. Open the guest's folio and navigate to the "Rate Details" tab (tab 4).

  2. Right-click and choose "Manual Posting." Select "Laundry Dry Cleaning" as the post code and enter the amount from the dry cleaning receipt. Click "NEXT."

  3. Note: It is not necessary to type the itemized list, as the original receipt will be given to the guest with their laundry.
