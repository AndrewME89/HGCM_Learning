Bond

A Security Deposit or Bond is required for every room. This is to ensure that if there is any damage to the room or incidentals to be paid for, the Bond can be used to cover those costs.

While it is always our preference to obtain a Pre Authorisation from a credit card, occasionally guests cannot provide this form of payment. In instances such as these, we can take the bond as a payment.

  • Cash = $300.00 per room.

  • Eftpos Card = $300.00 per room.

  • Smart Device = $300.00 per room.

A Duty Manager can approve a Bond of less than $300.00 if necessary.

If a guest is leaving a $300.00 bond for their stay, all other charges must be pre-paid before providing a key. This includes Accommodation, Upgrade fees, Rollaway fees, Prepaid Breakfast, etc

The ONLY exceptions to the above rules are:

  • ACB (all charges back) we do not require a copy of ID, a pre auth or bond, these guests are simply to be checked in and given their keys.

  • DFFH (Department of Families, Fairness & Housing), we do not require a copy of ID, a pre auth or bond; these guests are simply to be checked in and given their keys.

  • Shipping companies (Inchcape Shipping, Borthwick, China Navigation, Gulf Agency, etc.) are able to provide their passport to be held in our back office safe in lieu of a method payment. They can also choose to provide $100 pre-auth or bond. However, pre-auth may take up to 2 weeks to clear as guest may use international CC. Furthermore, all staff must ensure it is thoroughly explained to the guest exactly what the company will and will not cover for them and that any additional charges they have to settle on check out must be done with Australian dollars only.

Procedure - Cash Bond

Check In
  1. Inform the guest:

    2. a) To allow 5 minutes upon Check Out for the room to be checked.

    b) All F & B orders and other incidentals will need to be settled upon delivery.

  2. Collect a blank Cash Bond Form (pictured below) and fill in the relevant information in the Bond Amount Given section. Have the guest sign sign the document and staple it to the back of the Registration Card.

  3. In the Additional Items section of the guest folio, create a new item under the Stock code "Cash Guest".

  4. Enter "CASH GUEST $300" into the Description section.

  5. Check that the start (Check In) and end (Check Out) dates are correct.

  6. Go to the Transactions tab and right-click to create a Manual Posting.

  7. Change the Post Code to "Cash Bond" and the amount to $300, then click "Next".

  8. On the guest Registration Card, write "CASH BOND" in the top right corner.

  9. Fill out the guest details on the Cash Bond Envelope (pictured below).

  10. Enter a note in the Remarks & Check Out Instructions sections of the guest folio stating "Cash Bond $300 in B/O safe".

  11. Should a Cash guest extend their booking they are required to prepay their charges as they had before.

Check Out
  1. Inform the Duty Manager that the Cash guest is departing so that the room can be checked for damages.

  2. Once check has been completed, go to the Transactions tab and right-click to create a Manual Posting. Ensure the Post Code is set to "Cash Bond" and enter the amount as -$300.

  3. Count the money to be returned to the guest after all incidentals and charges have been paid.

  4. Remove the Cash Bond Form from the Registration card and fill in the relevant details in the Bond Amount Returned section. Have the guest sign the document and file it in the Back Office.

Procedure - Eftpos Bond

Check In
  1. Inform the guest:

    2. a) To allow 5 minutes upon Check Out for the room to be checked.

    b) All F & B orders and other incidentals will need to be settled upon delivery.

  2. In the Additional Items section of the guest folio, create a new item under the Stock code "Cash Guest".

  3. Enter "EFTPOS/VISA/MC/AMEX GUEST $300" into the Description section.

  4. Check that the start (Check In) and end (Check Out) dates are correct.

  5. Go to the Transactions tab and right-click to post a payment.

  6. Enter the amount of the Bond, ensuring the "opt out" checkbox is ticked.

  7. Check that the payment type is correct (Amex/Eftpos/Visa/Mastercard etc) and click "Next".

  8. On the guest Registration Card, write "EFTPOS/VISA/MC/AMEX BOND" in the top right corner.

  9. Enter a note in the Remarks & Check Out Instructions sections of the guest folio stating "EFTPOS/VISA/MC/AMEX GUEST $300".

Check Out
  1. Inform the Duty Manager of a Bond refund, and fill in the Security Bond Refund Form (pictured below)

  2. Process the refund as per Refund procedure.

  3. Post a refund of the remaining Bond amount in the Transactions tab.
