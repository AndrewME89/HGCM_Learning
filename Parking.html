Balancing VIP Parking

This procedure outlines how to log VIP car park registrations in iHMS and generate the necessary reports for the Duty Manager.

Logging VIP Car Park Entries
  1. Search for the guest's surname in iHMS, as indicated on the VIP Car Park sheet. Remember to check the "Sharing Guest" name as well.

  2. Open the guest's folio and go to the "Additional Items" tab.

  3. In the "STOCK" section, type "CAR" and use the keyboard arrows to select either "CAR PARK - CORPORATE" or "CAR PARK - GUEST." Press Enter.

    • To determine the correct parking type, check the "Rate Type" in the "Corporate" tab.

    • Rate types starting with "INT..." indicate "CAR PARK - GUEST."

    • Rate types starting with "COR..." indicate "CAR PARK - CORPORATE."

  4. Enter the car registration in the "DESCRIPTION" section.

    5. Generating Reports

    Once all guests from the VIP Car Park sheet have been entered into iHMS:

    1. Generate the "In-House Additional Item Report" for "Car Park - Guest":

      • Go to Reports → In House → In House Additional Item Report.

      • Select today's date and click "OK."

      • Double-click on "Car Park - Guest" and then "PRINT."

    2. Generate the "In-House Additional Item Report" for "Car Park - Corporate":

      • Repeat the steps above, but double-click on "Car Park - Corporate" instead.

      Note:

      Review all pages of these reports, as group guests might appear in the "Group" section towards the end.

    3. Generate the "Transaction for Business Day" report:

      • Go to Reports → Before Night Audit Reports → Transaction for Business Day.

      • Print the "Car Parking" section (if present).

    Report Handling
    1. Make a double-sided, color photocopy of the VIP Car Park sheet.

    2. Give the photocopy and other printouts to the Duty Manager.

    3. Place the original VIP Car Parking sheet on top of the water cooler or printer.
