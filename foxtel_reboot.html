Foxtel Reboot Procedure

Sky News - Channel 601
  1. Simultaneously hold down the BACK and CENTRE buttons on the remote until all lights illuminate and then turn off, leaving the standby light red.

  2. Press the POWER button. The standby light should change to green.

  3. Select the correct channel (601) using the DOWN arrow key five times.

  4. Press the CENTRE button twice to save the channel.

ESPN - 508, Lifestyle - 106, National Geographic - 612, Discovery - 610, Fox 8 - 108
  1. Hold down the STANDBY and FOXTEL buttons simultaneously. The screen will go blank and then display a green light moving in a circle. A small green light and a larger red light will appear.

  2. Wait for the red light to disappear, then press the STANDBY button again. The number 999 should appear.

  3. Set the desired channel using one of the following methods:

    • Press the UP arrow button slowly until the desired channel number appears. (Rapid pressing may cause the box to reset.)

    • Use the remote to select the channel, holding it close to the set-top box to avoid affecting other boxes.

Verification and Further Assistance

After resetting the boxes, confirm that the channels are working correctly on a TV in one of the rooms. If issues persist, contact Foxtel technical support at 1300 130 188.
