Business Travel Partner (BTP) Program

The Business Travel Partner Program is exclusive to HGCM and offers corporate guests/companies added benefits for booking with us directly.

Please see below all inclusions:

  • Availability and access to a fixed Business Travel Rate year round

  • Best Rate Guarantee

  • Breakfast for one person daily - Add breakfast for second person for just $22.00 per person per day (Retail Price $34.00)

  • High speed in-room Wi-Fi

  • Bottle of still water daily

  • 10% Food & Beverage discount when dining in Encore Restaurant & Bar

  • Every negotiated corporate guest receives a complimentary standard drink per stay at Encore

  • Restaurant & Bar

  • Book any event and the facilitator will receive a complimentary room upgrade to a Premier King

  • Room

  • Discounted Car Parking rate at V.I.P. Car Park at $30.00 per night (Retail Price $40.00)*

Additional Benefits

  • No annual minimum room night requirement - we just want to reward your loyalty!

  • Best Rate Guarantee - your contracted rate is the most you will pay. Should you find a lower rate promoted for the same room type, we'll match it and you get to keep your benefits (includes promotional rates)!

  • Loyalty Programs - earn points and benefits for staying with us

  • Rates are available for personal use

  • Rates are available to subsidiary companies

The following exclusions apply:

  • Shipping company guests are NOT entitled to the Encore discounts NOR the complimentary drink voucher - that is Inchcape, China Navigation Company (Swire), Borthwick Maritime Services and Gulf Agency Company (GAC).

  • Government guests are NOT entitled to the complimentary drink voucher (e.g. DHHS)

Note:

*Car parking space is strictly subject to availability, and spaces cannot be reserved in advance.
