Groups

Bookings extend beyond inbound travelers. Reservations for private gatherings, corporate events, and conferences involving four or more rooms can be categorized as group bookings.

Creating a Group Booking in HMS
  1. Access Group Booking Function
    • Press Alt + G or click the designated Group Booking icon.

  2. Enter Booking Details
    • Complete the required information and proceed to the next step.

      • Company: Verify if company details exist in the system. If the company is new, create a profile and ensure a payment account is set up.

      • Group Name: Use the name provided by the booker during the initial inquiry.

      • Check-in & Check-out Dates: Specify the group's arrival and departure dates.

      • Rate Type: Select the appropriate rate type for the booking.

      • Contact Person: Provide the booker's name and contact details.

      • Number of Rooms: Indicate the total number of rooms needed.

  3. Assign Market Segment
    • Click "Next" and adjust "Mkt Seg 4" to "Inb FIT" or "Inb Group" based on the number of rooms booked.

  4. Update Check-in Instructions
    • Add any specific check-in instructions pertaining to the group.

  5. Create Task for Follow-up
    • Right-click the "Special Instruction" tab under "Messages" and choose "Add Message." You can also right-click on the folio and select "Add Message."

    • Provide the following task details:

      • Type: Change to "Task."

      • Status: Set to "Open."

      • To: Assign the task to the appropriate department.

      • Date: Indicate the date for action or follow-up.

      • Message: Briefly add instructions or a reminder.

    • The created task will be visible on the folio.

  6. Generate and Send Confirmation
    • If accommodation payment is pending, create a group invoice to include with the confirmation email.

    • The invoice template is located at: M:\Operations\Reservations\Groups Invoice and Cover Sheets\Group Tax Invoice Template.

    • Fill in the required information and save the document in both Excel and PDF formats within M:\Operations\Reservations\Groups Invoice and Cover Sheets\GROUP INVOICES.

    • Send the confirmation to the booker, ensuring to attach the group invoice and hotel bank details for payment.

    • Find the hotel bank details at: M:\Operations\Reservations\Groups Invoice and Cover Sheets\BANK DETAILS.
